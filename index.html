About Me

During my time at school, I began to get carried away with the computer and its capabilities. As a teenager, I graduated from a music and art school. Later he graduated from high school and went to college. After college, I went to serve in the army. In Russia, military service is compulsory for men.

After the army, I went to university and started working at the same time. During my studies, I found a good international company where I worked and developed for 7 years.
